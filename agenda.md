## Recommended Teaching Timeline (≈6–7 hours)

* **Introduction & Setup** – 30 min
* **JSX, Components, Props** – 45 min
* **State & Events** – 60 min
* **Counter + Stopwatch** – 45 min
* **Lists, Forms, Conditional Rendering** – 60 min
* **Todo + Pizza Builder** – 60 min
* **`useEffect` + Fetch API** – 45 min
* **Dog Pictures** – 20 min
* **Routing** – 20 min
* **Tic Tac Toe / Whac-a-Mole** – 45 min
* **Deployment, Recap & Q&A** – 20 min

This sequence builds concepts incrementally so that each project introduces just one or two new ideas while reinforcing everything covered earlier.
